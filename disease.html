Common Cotton Diseases

Identify symptoms early and protect your cotton crop

High Severity

Bacterial Blight

Symptoms: Angular leaf spots, yellowing, leaf drop.

Cause: Bacterial infection through seeds and rain.

  • Use certified seeds
  • Avoid overhead irrigation
  • Copper fungicide spray
Predict
Medium Severity

Fusarium Wilt

Symptoms: Wilting, stunted growth, yellow leaves.

Cause: Soil-borne fungus.

  • Crop rotation
  • Resistant varieties
  • Improve drainage
Predict
Low Severity

Alternaria Leaf Spot

Symptoms: Brown circular spots.

Cause: Fungal infection in humid climate.

  • Field hygiene
  • Proper spacing
  • Recommended fungicide
Predict
Low Severity

Leaf Curl Virus

Symptoms: Curled and thickened leaves.

Cause: Whitefly-transmitted virus.

  • Whitefly control
  • Resistant seeds
  • Remove infected plants
