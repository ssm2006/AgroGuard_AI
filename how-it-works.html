How Cotton AI Works

A deep dive into how Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning work together to protect cotton crops.

Cotton AI is an intelligent decision-support system designed especially for farmers. It analyzes cotton leaf images using advanced AI models to identify diseases at an early stage, enabling timely treatment and reducing crop loss.

AI Disease Detection Workflow

1

Image Acquisition

Farmers capture cotton leaf images using a smartphone camera or upload images from their gallery.

2

Image Preprocessing

Images are resized, normalized, and enhanced using OpenCV to remove noise and improve clarity.

3

Feature Learning

The CNN model automatically learns patterns such as discoloration, texture, lesions, and leaf spots.

4

AI Prediction

A trained deep learning model predicts the disease category with confidence probability.

5

Smart Recommendation

The system provides disease severity, treatment suggestions, and preventive measures.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Cotton AI uses Explainable AI techniques to show why a prediction was made. Highlighted regions on the leaf indicate infected areas, increasing trust and transparency.
