About Cotton AI

AI-powered solution for healthy cotton farming

Project Overview

Cotton AI is an Artificial Intelligence based cotton disease prediction system designed to help farmers identify cotton leaf diseases at an early stage. The system uses Deep Learning and Computer Vision techniques to analyze cotton leaf images and provide accurate disease detection along with treatment recommendations.

Problem Statement

Cotton farmers often face heavy crop losses due to late disease detection and lack of expert guidance. Manual disease identification is time-consuming and requires agricultural expertise which is not always available.

Our Solution

Cotton AI provides a simple and affordable solution where farmers can upload a cotton leaf image and instantly receive disease information, severity level, and prevention methods using Artificial Intelligence.

Future Scope

  • Mobile application for farmers
  • Multi-language support (Hindi, Marathi, etc.)
  • Weather-based disease prediction
  • Expansion to other crops
